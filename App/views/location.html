Waihi Beach, New Zealand.

Waihi Beach is located on the border of the Coromandel and Bay of Plenty regions. Check out this site for some cool pics of the region

For those travelling across the ditch, the nearest airport is Auckland International. Waihi Beach is a 2 hr drive south-east of Auckland. The ceremony and reception location are shown on the map below.

View Larger Map

Please note that Waihi Beach is a small coastal settlement with only a few cafes and shops.

The nearest supermarket is in Waihi Township on State Highway 2 (you would pass through here on your way from Auckland) – it’s 11km west of Waihi Beach

The turn off for Waihi Beach is 2km south of Waihi town.
