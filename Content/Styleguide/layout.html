Wedding day

Ceremony

The ceremony will be on the beach infront of Leo Street. There will be an archway and a few chairs so you should be able to find it OK. If not, ask a friendly local!

Incase of terrible weather (unlikely but you never know!) we'll head straight up to the RSA and hold the ceremony there. We would have someone down at Leo St who can direct you up to the RSA if this is the case.

Following the ceremony, we'll have a few celebration drinkies on the beach and a few photos.

Reception

Its approx a 10 min walk from the beach to the reception venue, and it is up quite a steep hill, so keep that in mind when you're enjoying a few beers/ wine on the beach. If you prefer to drive, there is a large carpark at the RSA, where you can leave your car overnight.

Please indicate any special dietary requirements, when you RSVP. Especially those of you who particularly hate plants!

Bring your dancing shoes. We want to see an overall winner for the dancing chicken!

The night finishes at 12am so if you want to party on, make sure you've stocked up beforehand as Waihi is a small town and unlikely that you'll find a liquor store open that late!

All accommodation on the list (see Accom page) is within easy walking distance, but there is also a Courtesy Bus which can drop you home for $2.

Weekend Activities

We'll be in Waihi Beach from the Thursday arvo til Sunday. It would be great if you can stay around for the weekend, as we realise alot of you are travelling a long distance to help celebrate our day with us, so we want to be able to catchup properly.

Thursday

For those that are around, we will have a BBQ and to prepare for the big day we will be heading down to the Waihi Beach Hotel (pub) for a few brews.

Friday

Saying I do (Ceremony starts at 3pm).

Saturday

Most of us should will be suffering from party over indulgence after friday, so to remedy, we will be going for a morning dip at the beach. So come join us, we can get rid of the cobwebs together!

For breakfast, we will be going to a cool little cafe called Secret Garden, it's on Wilson Rd (main shopping strip). We'll be there from 11am so cruise in if you're in need of a coffee.

The afternoon we will spend down on the beach, maybe a picnic and some hurling and cricket games on the beach in front of Coronation Park.

Waihi Activities

At the northern end of Waihi beach, there is a lovely walk through forest and up over a hill to Orokawa Bay - well worth a look. perhaps take a picnic/snacks as it is a 40min walk each way.

There is some hot pools down at Athenree (web link...)

If the surf's up, join us in the waves. My parents will bring down some boogie boards. Don't forget to swim between the flags. The Phillips lifeguards will be around but prefer to remain off-duty!

Mount Maunganui is about 30-40 min drive from waihi and has cafe's, beach and a mountain walk.

Karangahake Gorge - the scenic drive between Paeroa and Waihi - there are some walks through the forest / along river ...see link...

Hahei - home of the famous Cathedral Cove (see link)
